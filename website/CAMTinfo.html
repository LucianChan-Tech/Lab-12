Basic Development and Operation

North Minzu University located in Yinchuan, Ningxia, China, NMU is a university focused on ethnic studies, culture, and education. It offers programs in humanities, sciences, engineering, and arts, with a special emphasis on serving China’s minority communities.

Chiang Mai University located in Chiang Mai, Thailand, CMU is a top public research university known for its strong programs in medicine, engineering, agriculture, and the arts. It is one of Thailand’s oldest and most prestigious universities, surrounded by beautiful mountain scenery.
